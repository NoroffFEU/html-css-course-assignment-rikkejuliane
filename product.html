ILMA PARKA

Black

Introducing Ilma, a timeless parka designed with a sporty aesthetic, providing full waterproof and windproof capabilities with sealed seams. Crafted from a soft and lightweight material, this parka ensures you're well-prepared for inclement and blustery weather conditions.

This parka features a sleek matte finish, equipped with four zippered front pockets and two interior pockets—one of which is zippered. It boasts an adjustable fixed hood, accompanied by a high collar for added protection. Both cuffs and waist are adjustable for a personalized fit. The two-way zip front offers ventilation, secured with a protective storm flap and discreet fastening.

Adorned with a reflective printed logo and taped details on the back, the lining of this parka is dyed using Solution Dye—a technique that conserves up to 80% of water compared to traditionally dyed polyester/polyamide fabric. Additionally, it is treated with a PFC-free water-repellent finish.

  • 100% polyester
  • Water Column: 10,000mm
  • Breathability: 5,000 g/m²/24h
